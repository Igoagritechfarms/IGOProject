Awards and Achievements

IGO Agritech Farms is a leading agricultural solutions provider that has made a significant impact in the industry. Over the years, the company has received numerous awards and recognition for its excellence in the field of agriculture.

One of the most notable awards that IGO Agritech Farms has received is the MSME Award, which recognizes outstanding micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises in India. The company was honored with this award for its contributions to the agricultural sector and its commitment to promoting sustainable agriculture practices.

In addition to the MSME Award, IGO Agritech Farms has also received the SISI award, which is presented to organizations that have made significant contributions to the industrial development of their region. The company was recognized for its efforts to promote agriculture and support local farmers.

IGO Agritech Farms has also been honored with the Trade award, which recognizes companies that have demonstrated excellence in international trade. The company was recognized for its export efforts and its contribution to the global agricultural industry.

The company has also received the Valluvam award​, which recognizes outstanding achievements in the field of agriculture. This award was presented to IGO Agritech Farms for its innovative solutions and contributions to the growth of the agricultural industry.
